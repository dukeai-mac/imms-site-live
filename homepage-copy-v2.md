## Section 3 — Why It Matters

### International Accounts Demand More Than Standard Billing Processes

Hospitals serving international patients often face a more complex revenue environment — one shaped by payer variability, documentation demands, time-sensitive communication, and the need for consistent follow-up.

Without specialized financial oversight, international departments can experience:

- avoidable revenue leakage
- delayed claims progression
- weak payer follow-up
- inconsistent documentation flow
- reduced visibility into account performance

IMMS helps hospitals address these challenges with a more disciplined and recovery-focused operating model.
