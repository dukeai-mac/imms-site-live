IMMS Brand System Exploration

Modern luxury institutional branding directions for IMMS.

The core objective is to stop relying on the legacy white-box logo as the main visual asset and instead create a brand language that feels established, international, refined, and web-native.

Direction 1 · Recommended

Modern Luxury Institutional

This is the strongest fit for IMMS. It balances premium restraint, executive credibility, and current web polish. The system is built around a clean wordmark, a disciplined color palette, and optional secondary marks rather than forcing a legacy logo everywhere.
