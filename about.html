About IMMS

Built to elevate the financial performance of hospital international departments.

IMMS helps hospitals improve international account performance through stronger financial discipline, better claims execution, more persistent recovery follow-through, and a more professional operating standard across the international dept.

International Multiple Medical Services (IMMS) was founded by Dr. Alberto Mena in 1998 with a clear understanding: international patient business requires a specialized operational and financial approach.

Hospitals serving international patients, travel-insured patients, expatriates, and assistance-backed cases face a level of billing and claims complexity that standard workflows often do not fully address. For nearly three decades, IMMS has helped hospitals navigate that complexity.

Foundation

Built on long-term specialized experience.

The company’s perspective has been shaped by years of work inside the real operational pressures of cross-border healthcare, insurer coordination, documentation flow, and financial recovery.

Our Mission

Help hospitals build stronger, more professional, and more financially effective international departments.

Our Role

Operate as a specialized extension of the hospital team, with the mindset of an International Billing Director rather than a generic outsourced vendor.

Our Perspective

Built for hospitals operating across borders, languages, payers, documentation dependencies, and complex international case environments.
