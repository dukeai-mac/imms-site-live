About IMMS

A specialized partner for hospital international departments.

IMMS helps hospitals strengthen the performance of their international dept. through greater financial discipline, stronger claims execution, more effective revenue recovery, and a higher standard of operational professionalism across international accounts.

International Multiple Medical Services (IMMS) was founded by Dr. Alberto Mena in 1998 with a clear understanding: international patient business requires a specialized operational and financial approach.

Hospitals serving international patients, travel-insured patients, expatriates, and assistance-backed cases face a level of billing and claims complexity that standard workflows often do not fully address. For nearly three decades, IMMS has helped hospitals navigate that complexity.

Foundation

Built on long-term specialized experience.
