# About IMMS — Draft v1

## About International Multiple Medical Services

International Multiple Medical Services (IMMS) is a specialized partner to hospitals seeking to strengthen the performance of their international dept. through greater financial discipline, stronger claims execution, and more effective revenue recovery across international accounts.

Founded by **Dr. Alberto Mena in 1998**, IMMS was built around a clear understanding: international patient business requires a specialized operational and financial approach. Hospitals serving international patients, travel-insured patients, expatriates, and assistance-backed cases face a level of billing and claims complexity that standard workflows often do not fully address.

For nearly three decades, IMMS has supported hospitals in navigating that complexity through focused expertise in verification of benefits, guarantee of payment workflows, billing support, claims preparation, payer follow-up, collections support, and reconciliation.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to help hospitals build stronger, more professional, and more financially effective international departments — ensuring international accounts are managed with the structure, persistence, and expertise required for better recovery and long-term growth.

## Our Approach

IMMS does not approach international billing as a generic back-office function. We operate as a specialized extension of the hospital team, helping international departments improve financial execution, reduce revenue leakage, and maintain a higher level of professionalism in the management of international accounts.

Our service model is built to be:

- **specialized** in the complexity of international patient business
- **customizable** to the structure and needs of each hospital
- **performance-aligned** so that, in many cases, our incentives remain directly tied to recovery results

## International Perspective

IMMS is designed to support hospitals operating in cross-border environments and serving international patient populations across multiple countries and markets. Our work is shaped by the practical realities of international tourism, travel-related care, payer variability, documentation requirements, and the need for timely coordination across institutions, insurers, and assistance companies.

Because international patient business is inherently cross-border, IMMS is being positioned from the start with multilingual communication and international accessibility in mind.

## Why Hospitals Work With IMMS

Hospitals choose IMMS when they want more than basic billing support. They choose IMMS when they need a specialized partner capable of bringing stronger financial oversight, more disciplined follow-up, and a more structured operating model to the international dept.

With nearly 30 years of experience and more than **$400 million billed to insurers and assistance companies**, IMMS brings the depth, alignment, and operational commitment required to support high-performing international departments.

## Looking Ahead

IMMS continues to evolve with the needs of hospitals serving international patients. As international healthcare markets become more connected and more competitive, hospitals need partners who understand both the operational details and the financial realities behind international patient revenue. IMMS exists to help meet that need.
