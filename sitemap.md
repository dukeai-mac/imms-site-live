# IMMS Website Sitemap

## Core Goal
Establish IMMS as a premium, credible, international specialist for hospitals seeking to strengthen the performance of their international dept.

## Strategic Requirements
- Must present IMMS as serving multiple countries and cross-border hospital operations.
- Must be designed from the start for full multilingual support.
- Content architecture should support full translation into Spanish, French, German, Russian, and additional languages later.
- Language handling should not be treated as an afterthought; copy structure should remain clean and translatable.
- Messaging must remain executive, premium, and internationally credible.

## Recommended Initial Site Structure

### 1. Home
Purpose:
- Establish credibility quickly
- Explain what IMMS does
- Position IMMS as a specialized international partner
- Drive consultation/contact

Suggested sections:
- Hero
- Credibility / proof bar
- What IMMS does
- Why international accounts require specialization
- How IMMS works with hospitals
- Why IMMS
- CTA

### 2. About IMMS
Purpose:
- Tell the company story
- Establish founder credibility
- Reinforce longevity, specialization, and philosophy
- Position IMMS as an experienced international operator

Suggested sections:
- Company introduction
- Founded by Dr. Alberto Mena in 1998
- Mission
- Operating philosophy
- International scope
- CTA

### 3. Services
Purpose:
- Explain service pillars clearly
- Show operational depth without sounding cluttered

Suggested sections:
- Intro
- Verification of benefits
- GOP workflows
- Billing and claims management
- Payer follow-up and collections support
- Reconciliation and financial visibility
- Custom engagement model

### 4. Why IMMS
Purpose:
- Differentiate IMMS from generic billing vendors
- Explain why international accounts require specialization
- Highlight alignment and experience

Suggested sections:
- Why international accounts are different
- Why hospitals choose IMMS
- Performance-aligned model
- Customizable support
- International expertise

### 5. Contact
Purpose:
- Enable hospitals to initiate contact easily
- Support consultation requests

Suggested sections:
- Contact intro
- Consultation CTA
- Basic inquiry form
- Office/contact details
- Preferred response expectations

## Optional Future Pages
- Leadership
- Case Studies
- FAQ
- Markets / Countries Served
- Resources / Insights

## Multilingual Requirements
- Initial architecture should support language-based routes or localized versions of all key pages.
- Translation should be human-reviewed or business-reviewed before publication.
- Core language priority candidates:
  - English
  - Spanish
  - French
  - German
  - Russian
- Additional languages can be added later based on target markets.
- Avoid idioms and slang in base copy to keep translation quality high.
- Use short, clear headings and modular sections for easier localization.

## Brand Positioning Reminders
- Company name: International Multiple Medical Services (IMMS)
- Tone: premium executive + international specialist
- Audience: hospital owners, administrators, international dept. leaders, finance stakeholders
- Position IMMS as a specialized extension of the hospital team, not a generic vendor
