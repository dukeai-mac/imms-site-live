# Services — Draft v1

## Services

### Specialized Financial and Operational Support for Hospital International Departments

International Multiple Medical Services (IMMS) provides specialized support to hospitals seeking stronger performance, tighter financial execution, and better revenue recovery across international accounts.

Our services are designed around the operational realities of international patient business — where payer variability, guarantee of payment requirements, documentation standards, and persistent follow-up directly affect financial outcomes.

Rather than offering generic billing support, IMMS focuses specifically on the workflows that matter most to the international dept.

---

## Verification of Benefits

Clear verification at the front end helps reduce avoidable downstream issues.

IMMS supports hospitals with verification of benefits processes that help clarify coverage, identify requirements, and improve financial visibility early in the case lifecycle. This work is essential to setting appropriate expectations, improving case handling, and reducing preventable delays later in billing and claims progression.

---

## Guarantee of Payment (GOP) Workflows

Guarantee of payment workflows require precision, timeliness, and disciplined coordination.

IMMS supports hospitals in managing GOP-related processes with greater structure — helping teams improve documentation flow, communication discipline, and operational follow-through across international cases involving insurers and assistance companies.

---

## International Billing Support

International billing requires a different level of oversight than standard patient accounts.

IMMS helps hospitals strengthen billing execution for international accounts through specialized support tailored to the requirements of travel insurers, assistance companies, and cross-border healthcare payers. Our objective is to help the international dept. operate with greater consistency, professionalism, and financial control.

---

## Claims Preparation and Management

Claims performance often depends on the quality and discipline of preparation.

IMMS supports the preparation, organization, and management of claims across international accounts, helping hospitals reduce avoidable friction caused by incomplete submissions, weak documentation handling, or inconsistent process execution. Stronger claims discipline improves the probability of timely and successful recovery.

---

## Payer and Assistance-Company Follow-Up

Revenue recovery often depends less on submission alone and more on persistent, informed follow-up.

IMMS provides structured support for payer and assistance-company follow-up, helping hospitals maintain momentum on open accounts, reduce stagnation, and push claims toward resolution. This is one of the most important functions in international revenue recovery, and one of the areas where operational discipline can make the greatest difference.

---

## Collections Support

International accounts often require sustained collections effort across complex payer environments.

IMMS supports hospitals with collections-oriented follow-up designed to improve recovery outcomes, reduce overlooked balances, and bring greater accountability to outstanding international accounts. Our work helps the international dept. maintain stronger control over recoverable revenue.

---

## Payment Coordination and Reconciliation

Financial performance also depends on how well payments are matched, tracked, and reconciled.

IMMS supports payment coordination and reconciliation processes that help hospitals improve visibility into what has been billed, what has been recovered, and what remains outstanding. Better reconciliation strengthens financial reporting, operational clarity, and account-level oversight.

---

## Custom Engagement Model

No two hospitals operate the same way.

IMMS works with each client according to its operational structure, internal capabilities, case mix, and priorities. Our model is designed to integrate as a specialized extension of the hospital team — bringing focused support where it is needed most.

In many cases, the engagement structure can also be performance-aligned, with service economics tied to recovery outcomes.
