## Why IMMS

### International Accounts Require Specialized Financial Execution

Hospitals serving international patients operate in a more complex financial environment than standard domestic patient workflows typically demand. International accounts often involve multiple stakeholders, variable payer expectations, guarantee of payment requirements, tighter documentation discipline, and more persistent follow-up across borders.

That complexity creates risk. Without a specialized operating approach, hospitals can face avoidable revenue leakage, slower claims progression, weaker collections performance, and reduced visibility into the true financial performance of the international dept.

IMMS exists to help hospitals address that challenge directly.
